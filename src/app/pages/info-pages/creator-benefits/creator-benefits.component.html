solstream TV Creator Benefits, Apply now

Monetize your content

As a content creator you are in control of your own content. solstream will not force you to share exclusive content on solstream TV. Interactions (such as donations or subscriptions) with your content will give you rewards. This implies you can use the solstream TV Platform as an additional source of revenue and promotion on top of your existing channels!

Instant and Transparent Transactions

Your rewards go straight into your wallet. You don’t have to wait for payouts as with traditional centralized video & streaming services. All transactions are visible on the blockchain and can be tracked. Every transaction includes an 8% transaction fee necessary to sustain the solstream TV ecosystem.

Close Collaboration with Foundation

Collaboration is key in making the solstream TV Platform succeed. As a content creator you’ll have direct access to the solstream Foundation Telegram channel. Here you can post experiences, ideas or innovations, so the foundation can help you realize creating a dream platform for content creators such as yourself.

Personalize your Room

The room is the space where you can manage your channel. You can upload videos, start streaming or have a look at content you have created in the past. In your room you have designated spot(s) which you can personalize for your own benefit. This can be used to further promote your identity or to advertize for more revenue.
